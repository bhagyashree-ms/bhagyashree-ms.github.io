Types Of Pillow

Comfort Redefined, Sleep Embraced

Kopema’s sleep-inducing pillows efficiently supplement the mattress collection. You can choose from Recron, Latex or Memory Foam as per your need.

Our Recron pillows are known for their soft and comfortable sleeping surface, and the ability to maintain their shape for long-lasting comfort.

Kopema’s Latex pillows are popular for their durability and cooling capabilities. Latex is also anti-fungal and hypoallergenic, thus ensuring that you have a good night’s rest.

Our Memory Foam pillows significantly improve your sleep quality and overall comfort, thus ensuring proper rest. They are known to alleviate pressure points in your neck, shoulder, and upper back.
